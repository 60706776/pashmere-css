The Brand

Extrafine Cashmere - 100% made in Italy

Pashmere brand is produced and distributed by the historical knitwear factory Galassia based in Perugia, founded by the nobleman Meno Galatioto Rosso and his wife Mariuccia in 1965.

The company is one of the most historical among the “Cashmere district”- area which surrounds Perugia in the Umbria region- and is well-known for its strong traditions and high quality craftsmanship of cashmere garments. To this day the company is still managed by the Galatioto family, now in its third generation.

Pashmere is the only brand in the world specialized in high quality extra thin knitwear “Made in Italy”. PASHMERE has chosen particular cashmere extrafine , light fineness 18-30 gauges, one of the most valuable and demanding materials in high quality knitwear

100% Made in Italy

Pashmere is a brand entirely “Made in Italy, starting from the design of the collections to the final stages of production is completely processed in-house, making products 100% “Made in Umbria” product. Furthermore it has long-standing collaborations with the finest Italian suppliers of cashmere yarn which are “Cariaggi” and “Loro Piana” in order to guarantee the best possible quality from the raw materials.

Il Lifestyle

Men and women who wear Pashmere want to brighten, distinguish and refresh their look with a refined product, which is both unique and innovative. Our aim is to reinvent your wardrobe through elegant, high quality, knitwear garments, with exclusive details that render unique those who wear Pashmere, unlike overly classic out dated looks

At all times during the day, from a business meeting, happy hour, to a formal dinner, it is essential to elegantly stand out from the crowd. This has been made possible thanks to the exclusive treatments which the finest yarns undergo to make collections distinct such as: nuances, mottling, coatings, laminating, inlays, knitting stiches, jacquard, natural dyes. Thanks to constant research and development on exclusive effects and the latest trends, the brand is known for its unique style.
